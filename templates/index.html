FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Can't find what you're looking for? Join the waitlist and we'll answer personally.

What is PlugZone?+
PlugZone is a local services marketplace that connects clients with skilled service providers near them — from barbers and nail techs to private chefs, house agents, cleaners and more.
Is it free to join the waitlist?+
Completely free. Joining the waitlist costs nothing and gets you early access when we launch, plus exclusive perks for early supporters.
Can I sign up as both a client and a provider?+
Yes. You can use PlugZone to find services and to offer your own skills — both roles are supported on one account.
Which cities will PlugZone launch in first?+
We are starting with Lagos, Abuja, and Port Harcourt, then expanding based on demand across Nigeria and Africa.
How does payment work?+
Payments are held securely on the platform until the job is completed. Once you confirm the job is done, the provider gets paid. No cash, no stress.
When does PlugZone launch?+
We are actively building. Join the waitlist to be first in line and get notified the moment we go live.
